Patrona Puppy

A destination for Dog lovers.

Hi! I'm Patrona. I have not ever imaged that I can see many beautiful creatures in my life

During our years of service in the army, we faced many challenges on difficult missions, inclement weather and homesickness.

However, Army dog did help us go through this tough day due to their friendly. Gradually, I realize that this creature is very beautiful and started to study about them with all of their characters.

I met Rex - a very smart dog in our corps. He is a intelligent and courage boy.

This smart boy accompanied with me in many mission and save my life so many times. He is not only a friend, he is my family.

I used to think just only me has this feeling but no, my comrades have the same feeling with their accompany. It's incredible.



Our army corps did garrison in many locations from Euroupe to NorthAmerica back to Asia which created many opportunities to see and research many type of animals in which dog gived me a plenty of interests.

This creature is so wonderfull which made me have an idea to explore more about them and share with my friend when I come back home.

After serving in the army, I returned and studied more about the dogs I had met and research for new breeds as well.

I did share about this with my friend - Chad and he is a vet doctor and he is so happy for the idea and support it.

That's the reason I published this website to share to share about my experience as well as puppy care tips for everyone with the consultance from Chad.


Thank you for visiting our website.

We hope you can find valuable information about dog here and please share with us more about your story with your friend.

If we cant find the answer for your question right here, please feel free to send us a question, we will go back to you very soon.
